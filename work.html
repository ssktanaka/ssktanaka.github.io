Feel free to contact me for freelance assignments.

I report on a variety of topics including real estate, dining trends, health, lifestyle trends. Most recently, I was a staff reporter for The Wall Street Journal. I have also written for The Charlotte Observer and The Raleigh News and Observer.

I have won several awards, including an award for Best Home and Design Feature 2014 from the National Association of Real Estate Editors for my story on meditation homes, and the Melcher Award presented by Duke University in 2013 for my three-part employment series. My profile on the late poet Maya Angelou ran on the front page of the inaugural issue of The Wall Street Journal’s luxury real estate section Mansion. I’ve written for WSJ blogs like Speakeasy and Developments; created interactive graphics and maps for WSJ.com; and appeared on WSJ Live video programs.
